RideWise

An all in one cycling assistant that helps riders plan safer routes, avoid theft hotspots, find secure locking locations, track bike health,
discover nearby trails, and connect with repair services — designed to support cyclists wherever they ride.

The Challenge

Bicycle theft is a major deterrent for everyday cyclists. Riders often avoid using their bikes for errands, commuting, or leisure because they dont feel confident leaving them unattended — even when locked.

UX Vision Statement

To design a digital cycling companion that not only helps reduce bicycle theft but also empowers riders with the tools they need to ride smarter, safer, and more confidently. Through features like secure parking suggestions, theft zone alerts, fitness tracking, bike health monitoring, trail discovery, and repair resources, the goal is to give cyclists peace of mind — whether they’re commuting through the city, exploring new routes, or simply locking their bike outside a café. Ultimately, this service aims to build a safer and more connected cycling experience that supports riders wherever their journey takes them.

Ridewise Dashboard and Map

Sign up and dive straight into your personalized dashboard—your hub that brings all your cycling essentials together. Instantly access safety tools, stats, maps, and routes in one streamlined interface built for riders who want speed, simplicity, and total control. With the RideWise map quickly toggle through live filters on the interactive map that shows secure storage spots, repair services, and real-time theft hotspots—so you always know where its safe to park or ride.

Nearby List View and Bike Setup and Serial Number Input

Easily browse a simple list of nearby trails, bike racks, and shops. Everything you need is organized and easy to find, so you can get where you're going without any hassle. Secure your ride from day one. Easily input your bikes serial number and details to activate anti-theft tracking and connect to a nationwide recovery network.

Bike Health and Maintenance

Keep your bike in top shape with alerts for wear-and-tear, performance stats, and reminders for check-ups or fixes—like a mechanic in your pocket. Track your fitness with dynamic graphs and real-time data on heart rate, calories burned, and ride durations. It’s your cycling journey, visualized.
